About the Hackathon

Hack2Impact is a 12-hour online hackathon designed specifically for high school students, though we welcome participants of all ages. Organized entirely by high schoolers, this event is your chance to create solutions that could shape tomorrow alongside peers who share your passion for innovation.

What to Expect

  • Exciting theme announcement at opening ceremony
  • Mentor support throughout development
  • Live presentations to expert judges
  • Networking with fellow young innovators

Who Can Participate

  • High school students (primary focus)
  • Teams of 1-4 people
  • All skill levels welcome
  • Global participation (online event)
